About UX Masterclass

What is the UX Masterclass?

The UX Masterclass is a bi-annual global user experience and design event that will be held in New York on 9 October 2014. Our speakers will engage and inspire attendees to think outside the ‘silo’ of their industry and approach challenges with a fresh perspective.

The UX Masterclass is a flagship event of the UXAlliance – an international network of 25 user experience firms from around the world. The UX Masterclass is being brought to New York by GfK, a partner of the UXAlliance.

Fresh ideas

Industry-leading user experience, market research, and design professionals will share innovative methods, case studies, and best practices. The cross-industry conference theme promises to stimulate ideas and host presentations that attendees won’t hear anywhere else. Our short presentation format will keep attendees engaged and moving quickly through the day, maximizing their time for the one-day event.

Network

This forum will be a great opportunity to exchange knowledge and best practices with professionals from all over the world. Unique to this conference, we bring together the heads of the top global user experience firms from 15 countries.

Who should attend the UX Masterclass

This year’s UX Masterclass brings together experts, authors, and thought-leaders from the field of user experience and market research from all over the world, and presents a unique platform to learn, exchange ideas, gain and share inspiration, and network.

Attendees who would find the UX Masterclass a must-attend:

  • Marketing professionals
  • Marketing Research professionals
  • Product Managers / Functional Leads
  • User Experience Professionals
  • User Interface Designers / Architects
  • Development Managers / Architects
  • Digital Marketing Professionals
  • Design Professionals
  • User / Usability / Market Researchers
  • Business / Industry Analysts
